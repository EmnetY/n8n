### a. Information from QuickBooks

When you authorize the Application, we may access data from your Intuit QuickBooks account, including but not limited to:

- Company information

- Invoices, bills, and transactions
- Accounting and financial metadata

The specific data accessed depends on the permissions you grant during authorization.

---

### b. Technical and Operational Data

We may collect limited technical information necessary to operate the Application, such as:

- API request metadata
- Workflow execution logs
- Error and performance metrics
- Timestamps and system identifiers

This information is used solely for operational, security, and debugging purposes.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the Application
- Execute workflows and automations authorized by you
- Synchronize data between QuickBooks and connected systems
- Monitor performance, reliability, and security
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do **not** use your data for advertising or resale.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

Data accessed from QuickBooks may be processed temporarily during workflow execution.

- We do not store QuickBooks data longer than necessary to provide the Application’s functionality
